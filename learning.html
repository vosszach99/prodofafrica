Tips and Tricks

  • Store essential oils in a cool, dark place to maintain their potency.
  • Mix carrier oils with essential oils for a nourishing skin treatment.
  • Use our shea butter as a natural moisturizer for dry skin and hair.
  • Create DIY scrubs using our products and household items like sugar or coffee grounds.
